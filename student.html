Welcome here!

We are especially happy that you, as a student, have found your way here – after all, you are the future of law! We hope that you want to get to know us better by attending one of our events, writing your thesis with us, or working as a summer associate.


Our events – an excellent opportunity for a first impression!

Are you a student curious about CMS Wistrand? Then we think you should take the opportunity to meet us at university career fairs or other student events. Feel free to check our calendar for the next opportunity.

It's a chance for you to learn a bit more about who we are, our culture, and how we work – and for us to get to know you and tell you more about how you can start your career at CMS Wistrand.
